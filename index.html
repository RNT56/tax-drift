Tax assumption & limitations

This tool assumes a flat tax on realized gains only. The default German preset is 26.375%, based on 25% capital gains tax plus solidarity surcharge. It does not model church tax, saver's allowance, partial ETF exemptions, loss offsets, broker-specific tax handling or automatic FX conversion. If you fetch a live price, make sure your buy price, current price and costs use the same displayed currency.

For source context, see PwC Germany tax summary and GermanPedia's German capital gains overview.
