          Tax assumption & limitations
          
The default German preset is 26.375%, based on 25% capital gains tax plus solidarity surcharge. German detailed mode can model church tax, saver allowance, stock and other loss pots, ETF partial exemptions, prior taxed Vorabpauschale, foreign tax credits, FIFO lots and manual/live FX assumptions. It remains a planning tool; broker and tax-office treatment can differ.

          
For source context, see PwC Germany tax summary and GermanPedia's German capital gains overview.
